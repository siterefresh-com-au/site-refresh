Good to know.

What counts as urgent?

An active leak, burst pipe, overflowing fixture, blocked only toilet or sudden loss of hot water may need a call rather than a form. If there is an immediate electrical or structural risk, make the area safe and contact the appropriate emergency service first.

Will I understand the work first?

That is the aim of the experience shown here: diagnose the likely cause, explain the recommended fix and agree on the work before starting.

Can I send a photo of the problem?

Yes. The live version of this job brief can accept useful photos. In this public demo, files stay on your device and nothing is uploaded.
